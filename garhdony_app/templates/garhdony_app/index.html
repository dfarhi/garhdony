Now it is ruined cinders and ashes, the royal palace and the Cathedral of Kelemen burned to the ground, the line of High Kings ended. Their leaders strive to rebuild, but the inexorable decline continues.

Now you join with other leaders and heroes of Garhdony at a quiet gathering in an ancient, secluded Kazkan barony. What path it takes is in your hands. Are you an ambitious noble, seeking to occupy the vacant throne? A wandering knight, protecting the weak from all who would prey upon them? A holy Eminent, seeking the blessing of the Ascended to turn aside the tide of decay?

Will this weekend bring a time of glory and power for you and your people? A time of peace and tranquility? A time of salvation ... or a time of ultimate doom?
