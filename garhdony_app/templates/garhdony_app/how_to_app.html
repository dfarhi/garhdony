Before You Apply

  • To play in Dogmas you must be free from 3pm on Friday, April 25, 2014 until 10pm on Sunday April 27.
  • All players will receive a detailed history of their character's life so far; if you are not comfortable reading and internalizing over 100 pages of material beforehand, it's best not to apply (we think it's fun reading, of course!).
  • The world of Dogmas has gods that virtually every person will pray to and honor; if you play, you'd likely participate in these ceremonies and prayer. Unfortunately, if you're not comfortable with that, it is probably best not to play.
  • You apply to Dogmas by filling out the casting questionaire. This does not guarantee you a spot; we will finish casting by February and let you know then. Filling out the casting questionaire also does not commit you to playing if cast; we will ask for a commitment after we have cast everyone.
